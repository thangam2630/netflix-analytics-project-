# Resume Addition - Netflix Data Analytics Project

Copy this section into your resume under "PROJECTS" (add it above or below
your BCA Portal project).

---

## Format for Resume (Word/Docx)

**Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard** | Python, SQL, Power BI
GitHub: github.com/thangam2630/netflix-analytics-project   [update with your repo link]

- Analyzed a 1,200-record Netflix content dataset using Python (Pandas) to
  clean missing values, engineer features (genre, duration, seasons), and
  uncover content trends across 16 countries and 10+ genres
- Wrote 10 SQL queries including window functions (YoY growth via LAG()) and
  subqueries to extract insights on content distribution, top directors,
  and country-wise genre trends
- Designed a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard with DAX measures, KPI
  cards, and slicers to visualize content type mix, rating distribution,
  and platform growth trends from 2008-2026
- Produced 7 data visualizations (Matplotlib/Seaborn) revealing key
  insights: 69% Movie / 31% TV Show split, US as the top content market,
  and TV-MA as the dominant content rating
